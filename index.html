About Mars

Physical Characteristics

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun, known for its distinctive red appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface. It has a diameter of approximately 6,792 kilometers.

Atmosphere

The Martian atmosphere is thin and composed primarily of carbon dioxide, with traces of nitrogen and argon. Surface pressure is less than 1% of Earth's.

Surface Features

Mars features the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, and a massive canyon system called Valles Marineris that stretches across the planet.

Mars Missions

2021

Perseverance Rover

NASA's most advanced Mars rover, searching for signs of ancient microbial life and collecting rock samples.

2018

InSight Lander

Studying the interior structure of Mars through seismic monitoring and heat flow measurements.

2012

Curiosity Rover

Exploring Gale Crater and analyzing Martian soil and rock samples to understand past habitability.
